# News App 

The News app offers users the ability to customize their news feed by selecting from a diverse range of categories and sources. Users can easily access the primary source to read the complete news articles. Furthermore, the app enables users to perform keyword searches across news articles from various categories, providing a comprehensive and tailored news experience.
